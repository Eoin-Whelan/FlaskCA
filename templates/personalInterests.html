Well, first thing's first; I've got about a billion interests/hobbies but there's a few I'm insanely passionate about:


First Up:

Charity Work!

Like my interest in Assistive Tech, my passions are in helping people. A part of my contribution to charitable organisations is my 24 hour marathon streams. These are 24 hour video game sessions that run the full day broadcasted on Twitch.

Last August I raised in aid of Trans Equality Network Ireland, Enable Ireland (For the Assistive Technology division!) and I matched donations to €100 to go towards Direct Provision Donations, a student-created group that donate essential supplies to refugees living in Direct Provision centres across Ireland.


Raised over €800 so that meant the world knowing the support is there once someone's thrown something together.


Secondly, you might've guessed it:

Games!

My interest in games starts like most folks from a young age. First game was DOOM back on the PS1 and kept on trucking up until I started college and suddenly I've got no time! No but for real, my love is still very much there and using it as a platform for my charity is just a way to turn a time-wasting activity into something constructive and helping folks. I love both video and board games, and recently (Pre second lockdown!) my friends and I were really digging the XCOM board game.
